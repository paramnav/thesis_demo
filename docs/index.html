Interactive Experiments

Companion interactive visualizations for the dissertation on mapping Total Organic Carbon (TOC) and Mass Accumulation Rates (MAR). Data source for this build: synthetic shelf-to-deep-sea transect.

  • Experiment 1
    3D surfaces + 2D maps — RF, MLP (PyTorch), and k-NN combined with outlier drop targets
  • Experiment 2
    k-NN scaling dynamics & extrapolation dilemma — toggle hidden validation points
  • Experiment 3
    Random forest structural evolution — 5-stage complexity narrative slider
  • Experiment 4
    MLP projection surface constraints — architecture & activation progression
  • Experiment 5
    Information gain — target scale, relative uncertainty, and constant uncertainty
  • Experiment 6
    MSE / MAE / Huber loss slider — outlier sensitivity in 1D MLP
  • Experiment 7
    ReLU / tanh / logistic activation slider (1D extrapolation)
  • Experiment 8
    Covariate-shift marginals p_tr vs p_tg (with train/test point toggles)
  • Experiment 9
    MLP training lab with neuron activations and TOC/loss
  • Experiment 10
    Multi-task learning (MTL) vs Single-task with two targets (Regularization effect)

To use your Baltic CSV, copy data_config.example.json to data_config.json, set csv_path, and run python build_experiments.py again.
